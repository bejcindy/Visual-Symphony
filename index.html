VISUAL SYMPHONY is a collaboration project that would resolve as a video exhibition taking place at Central Saint Martins, University of London on May 3rd from 10 am to 4 pm. The exhibition features 10 big screens, paired with several headphones for the audience to use, allowing for a fully immersive experience. It incorporates tailored original compositions for prerecorded videos. Unlike traditional exhibitions or concerts, it dispenses with the conventional stage and the rigid division between performers and the audience. Rather than having a traditional stage, audience members are totally immersed in the art they're experiencing with soundproof headphones, removing them from the present world they are in, and allowing them to escape outside stresses and just be in the art in front of them. This event features 10 original experimental art films by artists from UAL (University of Arts London), collaborating with the Composers from NEC (New England Conservatory) and Berklee College of Music. Please join us for an unforgettable experience that will return year after year, growing into a beloved annual tradition!
