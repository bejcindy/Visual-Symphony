VISUAL SYMPHONY 2025 is a student-initiated and student-organized collaborative exhibition and showcase that bridges the creative communities of the United States, China and the United Kingdom. The project explores the intersection of experimental film, visual arts, and music composition, bringing together emerging visual artists and composers to create works that merge sound and image in innovative and immersive ways.

Following the success of Visual Symphony 2024—held at Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts London—the 2025 edition will continue this international dialogue at the University of California, Riverside. The exhibition celebrates interdisciplinary collaboration, cultural exchange, and the boundless possibilities that arise when music and visuals come together as one unified art form.
